Who We Are

A Hub for Computational Biology at KAUST

The KAUST Bioinformatics Platform provides state-of-the-art computational resources and expertise to support research in genomics, transcriptomics, metagenomics, and other data-intensive biological fields.

We are dedicated to accelerating scientific discovery through innovative data analysis, reproducible workflows, and intensive training programs for researchers and students across the university and beyond.

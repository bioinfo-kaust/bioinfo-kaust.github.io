About Us

The KAUST Bioinformatics Platform provides state-of-the-art computational resources and expertise to support research in genomics, and other bioinformatics-intensive fields. We are dedicated to accelerating scientific discovery through innovative data analysis and interpretation. We also provide intensive training workshops for researchers and students.

Our Services

  • Transcriptomics and RNA-Seq
  • Custom Bioinformatics Workflows
  • Training and Workshops
  • Support Bioscience Core Lab
